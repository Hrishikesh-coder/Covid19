WHAT IS THIS WEBSITE ABOUT ?

This website is about creating awareness in people's mind against the deadly virus nCoV-19 which has maimed and crippled the society. It has forced human beings into remote existence. However, we are hopeful that we shall overcome and better times shall come . This website is nothing but a simple attempt to create awareness among the masses about this disease .

WHAT DO WE DO ?

We show real-time data and analysis of the affected people all over the world . We have broken down the data into multiple formats with specific data for each country which allows a layman to understand whats actually going in any particular country around the globe. Not only that, we also provide reliable news that our user can trust on related to COVID-19 . Undoubtedly, this website is your one-stop guide for COVID-19 information.

FROM WHERE DO WE GET THIS DATA ?

Every effort has been made to avoid any kind of error in the data in this website. This data is a compilation of different kind of data available on the official websites of all countries. This compilation is completely done by our web scrapping algorithm with no human intervention. Utmost level of transparency is maintained with no censorship.
