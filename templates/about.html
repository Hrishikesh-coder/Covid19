WHAT IS THIS WEBSITE ABOUT ?

This website is about creating awareness in people's mind against this deadly virus know as Coronavirus , whose scientific name is SARS-CoV2 . This virus has affected almost every country in the world and has forced human beings into remote existence . However, we are hopeful that we shall overcome and better times shall come . This website is nothing but a simple attempt to create awareness among the masses about this disease .

WHAT DO WE DO ?

We show real-time data and analysis of the affested, recovered and deceased people all over the world . We have broken down the data into country-wise formats with specific data for each country. Not only that, we also provide reliable news that our user can trust on related to COVID-19 . Undoubtedly, this website is your one-stop guide for COVID-19 information.
